Chelsea


Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club based in Fullham, London and
England. They have won 7 FA cups, 7 leaugue cups,
4 FA community shields, 1 UEFA champions league, 2 UEFA cup winners 1 UEFA Europa league and 1 UEFA super cup..

The club was founded in 1905 and stamford bridge is their main stadium.

Antonio Conte is an Italian football manager and is currently the head coach of Chelsea.

Roman Abramovich the owner of the Chelsea Football Club and is 1 of the richest men in the world.
